→ Specific items for Summer?

Breathable fabrics like cotton or linen, shorts, skirts, and light dresses, sandals or lightweight walking shoes.
Sun Protection: Wide-brimmed hat, High SPF sunscreen, After-sun lotion or aloe vera gel.
Cooling Accessories: Portable hand fan, Cooling towels.

→ Specific items for Winter?

Warm Clothing: Coat or insulated jacket, Layers (sweaters, thermals, and long-sleeve shirts), Gloves, scarf, and a beanie.
Rain Protection: Compact umbrella, Waterproof boots or shoes.
